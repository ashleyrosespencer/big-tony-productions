Hells Canyon Ride 2022

A little GoPro short showing a some of our ride out to Hells Canyon and back. Close to 1000 miles in three days of riding, featuring incredible roads, unidentified large birds in the road, a foot of snow, torrential rains, cool camping, great air bnb's, atv festivals, hole in the wall cafes, and taverns & saloons you'd never find any other way. Filmed and edited May 2022.
